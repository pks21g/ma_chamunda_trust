About US:

Ma Chamunda Charitable Trust,we focus mainly on young and teen education. It provides free resources to those that are in need. It provides guidance and mentorships, in various skills and streams.

What we do

  1. At Ma Chamunda Charitable Trust, we empower children to excel in most things either technology, Science, Math or Arts.
  2. We provide long-term mentorship, guidance and support in their academic and professional journey to those who stay connected with us.

How we are run

We are solely run with our own resources and not affiliated to any agencies, either governmental, private or Non-profits. We work passionately to provide quality education in the field of Science, Technology, Languages, Maths, Arts, amongst many other.

Affiliation and Alignment

While we are not associated with any governmental or non governmental bodies, we are aligned with governments vision of digital India, digital literacy and Swaksha Bharat. We truly believe that everybody without any exclusivity, should be able to benefit from the technological advancement. People must be able to use all the technologies.

What we believe

Ultimately, we are an organization that truly believes in free quality education, educational resources, guidance and support system for the youth (the nation builders) for their academic and professional success and growth.

Mission Statement:

Nation building is our ultimate vision for the future. We plan to achieve that by providing truly a quality education to the younger generation in their desired field either by ourselves or by finding them a guiding force or a mentoring facility. While we are very serious in achieving our goals we truly want a momentum. We hope to inspire governmental and non-governmental bodies to make quality education a major priority.
