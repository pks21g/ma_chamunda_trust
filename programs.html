Programs

At Ma Chamunda Charitable Trust, our programs are designed to empower underprivileged children and youth through education, skills, and values. Inspired by the strength and compassion of Maa Chamunda, each initiative focuses on enabling self-reliance, confidence, and holistic development.

English Support Program

This program provides academic assistance to needy students through books, study materials, tuition support, mentoring, and examination guidance. The aim is to ensure continuity in education and reduce dropout rates among children from economically weaker sections.

Digital Literacy and Technology Access Program

Recognizing the importance of digital education, this program focuses on improving digital literacy and access to technology. The Trust facilitates the availability of computers, laptops, tablets, mobile devices, and basic IT training for students who lack such resources.

Skill Development and Hands on Learning

This program helps students and youth develop practical skills relevant to academics, technology, and employability. Career awareness sessions, basic vocational training, and guidance for future education pathways are offered to support informed decision-making.

Value Based Eduation and Cultural Awareness program

Inspired by India’s cultural heritage and ethical traditions, this program emphasizes values such as discipline, respect, integrity, and social responsibility. Students are encouraged to develop character, cultural awareness, and a sense of duty toward society.
