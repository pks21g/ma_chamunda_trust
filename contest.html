Saraswati Mata Talent Contest

The Saraswati Mata Talent Contest is a celebration of art, music, and culture, created to encourage students and talented youth by giving them a platform to showcase their skills. Participants compete for a grand cash prize, along with a momento and certificate, while every participant receives a certificate of participation. Esteemed judges from various fields evaluate the performances, and the trustee presents the awards and honors both the winners and judges for their valuable contributions.
