Donations

At Ma Chamunda Charitable Trust, every contribution becomes a source of strength and opportunity for students in need. Inspired by the spirit of seva and guided by the values of Maa Chamunda, your support—whether financial or in kind—helps us provide education, resources, and guidance to underprivileged children and youth.

Types of Donations We Accept

Monetary Donations

Financial contributions enable us to sustain and expand our educational programs. We accept monetary donations through the following modes:
  • Cash
  • Cheque
  • Bank Deposits and Bank transfers (NEFT / RTGS / IMPS)
  • Online Payments and digital modes
Online payments and digital modes All monetary donations are utilized solely for charitable and educational purposes in accordance with applicable laws. Donate Money

Technology and Digital Support Donation

To bridge the digital divide, we accept donations of technology items that support learning and digital access, including:
  • Computers, laptops and tablets
  • Mobile phones and smart devices
  • Computer accessories (keyboards, mouse, headphones, webcams)
  • Printers, scanners, routers and networking devices
  • Educational Software and digital learning tools
Any donations are weclome(new, used, outdate, unusable) Donate Tech

Educational and Learning Material Donations

We also welcome donations of educational materials that directly benefit students, such as:
  • Text books and reference books
  • Notebooks, stationey and school supplies
  • Study guide and exam preperation material
  • Educational kits and learning aids
These contributions help reduce the financial burden on students and their families. Donate Ed Material

Other Support That Empower Students

Any item, resource, or support that contributes to a student’s education, skill development, or overall growth is welcome. If you are unsure whether your contribution is suitable, our team will be happy to guide you.

Transparency & Utilization

Ma Chamunda Charitable Trust is committed to transparency, accountability, and ethical utilization of all donations. Contributions received are recorded, monitored, and used responsibly to maximize educational impact.

Your support is not just a donation—it is a step toward empowering education and shaping a better future. Join us in our mission of seva and learning.
