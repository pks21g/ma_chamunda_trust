About US:

Ma Chamunda Charitable Trust is a non-profit educational organization inspired by the ideals of Maa Chamunda—strength, compassion, and righteous service. Established with the purpose of serving society through education, the Trust works to support underprivileged and needy children and youth by providing educational assistance, learning resources, skill development opportunities, digital literacy, and value-based guidance. Rooted in the spirit of seva and inclusiveness, the Trust operates without discrimination and is committed to transparency, ethical governance, and the responsible use of resources to nurture self-reliant individuals and contribute to the holistic development of society.

What we do

Ma Chamunda Charitable Trust works to support underprivileged and needy children and youth through education, skill development, digital literacy, and value-based learning. The Trust provides academic assistance, learning resources, books, educational devices, and guidance to students from economically weaker sections, enabling them to pursue education with dignity and confidence.

How we are run

Ma Chamunda Charitable Trust is a non-profit organization governed by its trustees and managed in accordance with applicable laws and charitable principles. The Trust follows transparent governance practices, maintains proper financial records, and ensures that all resources are utilized responsibly and solely for charitable and educational purposes.

Affiliation and alignment

The Trust operates in alignment with national educational objectives and social welfare initiatives. Its activities are designed to complement formal and non-formal education systems and support the broader goals of inclusive education, skill development, and social upliftment in India.

What we believe

We believe that education is a powerful tool for social transformation. Every child deserves equal access to learning opportunities, irrespective of economic or social background. We believe in nurturing not only academic excellence but also ethical values, cultural awareness, self-reliance, and social responsibility.

Mission Statement

To empower underprivileged and needy students through education, skill development, digital literacy, and value-based guidance, enabling them to become self-reliant, confident, and responsible members of society.

Vision Statement

To build an inclusive and empowered society where every child and youth has access to quality education and opportunities for holistic development.
